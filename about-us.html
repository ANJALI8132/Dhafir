About Us

Why Dhafir Technologies

Dhafir Technologies LLC is part of Dhafir Group which was established in 1970. Initially the company’s activities were focused on in-house contracts under its parent company Dhafir Group.

In 1992 Dhafir Technologies diverged into an independent profit center to spearhead the rising demand for construction projects of Power, Oil & Gas sector within the UAE. Today, with three fabrication facilities, full-fledged engineering, Procurement, QA/QC and HS& E depart we have grown to hold a prominent position in the EPC contracting companies in UAE

Our Mission

  • To provide excellent services with contemporary technology and systems to Power, oil & gas and other utility industries
  • To maintain our position as a leading EPC contractor in the UAE.
  • To generate profitable growth to our share holders

Our Vision

Dhafir Technologies LLC aims to provide exemplary services in Engineering Procurement and Construction. Our credo is to build perfect solutions at optimal cost.
