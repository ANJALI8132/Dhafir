DHAFIR Technologies L.L.C

Dhafir Technologies is an EPC contractor executing turnkey projects in Oil & Gas, Power Water, Utility and Aviation industry Dhafir Technologies LLC is part of Dhafir Group which was established in 1970. Initially the company's activities were focused on in-house contracts under its parent company Dhafir Group. In 1992 Dhafir Technologies diverged into an independent profit center to spearhead the rising demand for construction projects of Power, Oil & Gas sector within the UAE.
